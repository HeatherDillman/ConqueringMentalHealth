Dealing with Anxiety

Living with Anxiety can be difficult, here's some tips to help you deal with it more effectively.

Engage in Soothing Activities:
1.) Listen to sounds like music you enjoy or sounds of nature
2.) Smells of incense, candles, or any other form of aromatherapy
3.) Sights that are aesthetically pleasing, such as beaches or falling water
4.) Nature activities such as hiking, swimming, parks, etc.

Body-Based Relaxation Strategies:
1.) Slow-paced breathing
2.) Yoga
3.) Tensing and relaxing your muscles
4.) Massages
5.) Hot tubs, baths, or a sauna

Mind-Based Relaxation Strategies:
1.) Meditation
2.) Body awareness
3.) Prayer
4.) Autogenics


What to do when you're having a panic attack


Deep Breathing. Deep breathing can counter the hyperventilations that come with a panic attack.

Breathe in. Count to 4. Breathe out. Count to 4.

Recognize you are having a panic attack. Simply just recognizing it can help you gain control of the situation. You will realize that you are okay and that the feeling will pass.

Close your eyes. Panic attacks can be caused by overstimulation. Closing your eyes can take away from busy environments.

Be mindful. Think about familiar situations, like picking grass from the ground or the feeling of dirt in your hands. Being familiar with situations can assist in focus.

Focus on one object. Note everything about it. The look, the sound it makes, the way it moves, the color, etc. This can divert your symptoms.
